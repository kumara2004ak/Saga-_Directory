Domain Verification Required

To verify domain ownership, you need to add DNS records to your domain's DNS settings. This process typically takes 5-10 minutes but can take up to 48 hours to propagate.

VERIFICATION STEPS

Sign in to your domain registrar or DNS hosting provider
Navigate to the DNS management section for your domain
Add the TXT records shown in the "DNS Records" tab
Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-10 minutes, up to 48 hours)
Click "Verify Domain" to complete the verification process
